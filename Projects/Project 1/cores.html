Titan RTX 72
  • What: The new RT cores are the backbone of these new RTX cards. These cores focus solely on Ray Tracing calculations, the new technology added into the RTX series cards. The RT cores allow the card to split up the processing, allowing for faster and more streamlined calculations.
  • Why: With the addition of the new technology, nVidia needed a way to keep all of the new calculations seperate, thus RT cores were created and help improve the overall performance of the RTX cards.
